What Is Lindy AI?

Lindy AI is an AI agent platform that lets you build and deploy agents for tasks like email drafting, meeting scheduling, customer support, and sales outreach. One of Lindy's standout features is its AI phone agent, which can make and receive calls on your behalf.

Lindy uses a credit-based pricing model. Each action an agent takes — sending an email, making a decision, calling an API — consumes credits. The free tier includes 400 credits, and paid plans offer increasing credit allotments. This means your monthly cost depends on how much your agents do, which can make budgeting unpredictable for heavy usage.

What Is AI OS?

AI OS is a Virtual Corporate Headquarters — a pre-built, fully-staffed AI company with 51 specialized agents organized into 10 departments (Executive, Engineering, Marketing, Creative, Legal, Customer Service, IT, Product, Operations, and a Board of Directors). Each agent has a name, an avatar, a defined role, and a specific model tier optimized for their job.

AI OS is open-source and self-hosted. The Community edition is free with 15 agents. Commercial licenses are a one-time purchase — no recurring fees, no per-action costs. The platform uses 10 model tiers to automatically route each task to the optimal AI model.

Key Differences

  • Pricing predictability: AI OS uses one-time license fees with no recurring costs. Lindy's credit-based model means costs scale with usage, making it harder to predict monthly spend.
  • Organizational structure: AI OS includes a full org chart with 10 departments, reporting lines, and named employees with avatars. Lindy treats agents as independent automations without organizational hierarchy.
  • Phone agents: Lindy offers AI phone agents that can make and receive voice calls. AI OS does not currently include voice calling agents.
  • Pre-built vs. build-your-own: AI OS provides 51 ready-to-use agents covering common business functions. Lindy requires you to build and configure each agent for your specific use case.
  • White-label: AI OS offers white-label with custom branding on the Business license. Lindy does not offer a white-label product.

Who Should Choose Lindy AI?

Lindy AI is a solid choice if you need AI phone agents for voice calls, prefer to build custom agents for very specific workflows, or have relatively light usage that stays within credit limits. It works well for sales teams that need automated outreach with phone follow-ups, and for users who want to start small and scale usage gradually. If voice calling is a core requirement, Lindy currently has an advantage in that area.

Who Should Choose AI OS?

AI OS is built for businesses that want predictable costs and a comprehensive AI workforce without building anything. With one-time license pricing, you know exactly what you'll spend — there are no recurring fees. The self-hosted model gives you full control, and the white-label option on the Business license lets agencies resell the platform under their own brand.
