What We Do

AI OS is a Virtual Corporate Headquarters — a complete AI-powered company staffed by 51 specialized agents organized into 10 departments. From a CEO who orchestrates strategy to a Legal department that handles licensing, from an SEO Agency that audits domains with 5 parallel sub-agents to a Creative Studio powered by Gemini Omni that generates video, images, and audio.

Unlike single-purpose AI tools, AI OS provides an entire organizational structure. Each agent has a name, a role, a department, a reporting line, and a specific model tier optimized for their job. The Orchestrator (Atlas, our CEO) routes work to the right agent at the right cost — strategic decisions go to Opus 4.8 at maximum reasoning depth, routine lookups go to low-effort scouts, creative generation goes to Gemini Omni, and bulk processing goes to DeepSeek economy tier.

How It Works

Clone the repo, add your API keys, and deploy on your own infrastructure. AI OS provides the orchestration layer — the 51-agent fleet, the dashboard, the SEO Agency, the Creative Studio, the Virtual HQ — while you bring your own model credentials. This means you control your own costs, rate limits, and data.

The Community edition is free and open-source. Business ($1,997 one-time) and Enterprise ($4,997 one-time) licenses add white-label branding, multi-tenant support, and more. Enterprise includes 1 year priority support, custom agents, and SLA with an optional $997/yr renewal — your company name, your logo, your colors, your domain.
