Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We respond to all emails within 24 hours on business days (Monday-Friday). Enterprise and Founders customers with active support have SLA-backed response times via their dedicated Slack channel.

I'm having trouble deploying. Where do I start?

Check the Deployment Guide first. If you're still stuck, email support@aiosorchestrationlab.com with your error output and we'll help you debug.

Can I upgrade from Community to Business or Enterprise?

Yes. Visit the pricing page and purchase a license. Your existing data and configuration are preserved — commercial features activate immediately when you add your license key.

I want to white-label AI OS for my agency. Who do I talk to?

Email support@aiosorchestrationlab.com with your use case. White-label branding is included in Business ($1,997) and Enterprise ($4,997) licenses. We can walk you through multi-tenant setup and custom domain configuration.

Is there a Founders Lifetime License still available?

Limited to 100 spots at $9,997 one-time. Email support@aiosorchestrationlab.com to check availability. Includes lifetime priority support with no annual renewal.

How do I report a security vulnerability?

Please email support@aiosorchestrationlab.com with details. Do not open a public GitHub issue for security vulnerabilities. We take all reports seriously and will respond within 24 hours.
