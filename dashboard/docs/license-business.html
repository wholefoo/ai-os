4. White-Label Rights

The Business License grants Licensee full white-label rights to the AI OS platform:

  • “Powered by AI OS” attribution is NOT required. You may remove all references to AI OS from the user-facing interface.

  • White-label configuration is managed through the platform’s admin settings panel.

The Business License permits multi-tenant operation:

  • You may create and manage multiple tenant instances for your clients within your single production deployment.
  • Each tenant receives isolated data storage, branding configuration, and feature settings.
  • There is no limit on the number of tenants you may create within your licensed deployment.
  • You are responsible for managing tenant access, onboarding, and support for your own clients.

  • Compliance with applicable data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) as they pertain to your deployment and your clients’ data.
