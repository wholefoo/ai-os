Getting Started

Get up and running with AI OS in under five minutes. Choose a plan, configure your API keys, and start orchestrating your Virtual Corporate HQ.

Step 1 — Choose a Plan

Visit aiosorchestrationlab.com and select the plan that fits your needs:

Free$0Limited dashboard, 3 agents, 1 SEO audit/mo, Atlas tour guide, Virtual HQ preview
Starter$49/mo15 agents, 3 SEO audits/mo, 5 departments, email support
Pro$99/moAll 51 agents, full Virtual HQ, SEO Agency, knowledge vault
Business$497/mo+ Gemini Omni Studio, YouTube Intel, white-label branding, subdomain
Enterprise$997/mo+ Custom domain, SSO, dedicated tenant, API access, SLA support
Founders$9,997Lifetime Enterprise Subscription. Everything forever. Limited to 100 spots.

Step 2 — Log In

After subscribing, log in to your dashboard at aiosorchestrationlab.com/app. Business and Enterprise customers get their own branded URL (e.g., yourcompany.aiosorchestrationlab.com).

Step 3 — Configure Your API Keys

AI OS uses your own API keys — you control spend and rate limits. Navigate to Settings in the dashboard sidebar and enter your keys:

Each key field has a Test button — click it after saving to verify the connection works.

Step 4 — Explore Your Virtual HQ

Click Virtual HQ in the sidebar to see your corporate headquarters — 51 named AI employees across 10 departments:

  • Executive Office — Atlas (CEO), Nova (CTO), Ledger (CFO), Meridian (COO)
  • Engineering — Forge, Prism, Flux, Phantom, Relay
  • Marketing & Sales — Echo, Quill, Beacon, Pulse, Catalyst
  • Creative Studio — Muse, Pixel, Reel, Vertex, Sonance, Palette
  • Customer Service — Harbor, Compass, Resolve
  • Tech Support — Matrix, Root, Guide
  • Legal — Justice, Shield, Covenant, Clause
  • Product & Innovation — Horizon, Oracle, Forecast, Archive
  • Operations — Hermes, Tempo, Gatekeeper, Ranger, Conveyor, Hawkeye
  • Board of Directors — Sentinel, Aegis, Cipher

Click any employee to view their profile, role description, and dispatch tasks directly.

Step 5 — Run Your First SEO Audit

Navigate to SEO Agency, enter any domain, and click Audit. Five sub-agents run in parallel:

  1. Keyword Analysis
  2. Technical SEO
  3. Competitor Analysis
  4. Content Analysis
  5. Backlink Profile

After the audit completes, you get a composite score, executive summary, quick wins, and a phased action plan. Use the post-audit buttons to generate content briefs, a 12-week calendar, or optimized meta tags.
