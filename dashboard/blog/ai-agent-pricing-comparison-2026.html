AI Agent Platform Pricing Comparison 2026: AI OS vs Lety.ai vs Stammer.ai vs MindStudio

June 2026 12 min read Comparison

Choosing an AI agent platform in 2026 is harder than it looks. Pricing models range from free open-source to monthly SaaS subscriptions to one-time license purchases, agent counts range from 1 to 51, and model support ranges from a single LLM to 10 auto-routed tiers. This guide puts AI OS, Lety.ai, Stammer.ai, and MindStudio side by side so you can find the right fit for your business.

Why Pricing Comparisons Are Complicated

AI agent platforms charge in fundamentally different ways. Some bill per message. Some charge a flat monthly fee. Some are SaaS with your API keys (BYOK), others markup compute and keep the margin. Before you compare numbers, you need to understand what you are actually buying:

  • Platform fee — what you pay for the orchestration layer, dashboard, and support
  • Compute cost — what you pay for the underlying models (passed through or marked up)
  • Agent count — how many specialized agents come prebuilt vs. ones you have to configure yourself
  • Model breadth — how many different LLMs and tiers are available
  • White-label rights — whether you can rebrand and resell the platform
  • Setup time — how long before you are actually running tasks

With those dimensions in mind, here is what each platform actually offers in 2026.

Platform Overviews

AI OS Orchestration Lab

AI OS is a full AI operating system built around the concept of a Virtual Corporate HQ — 51 named AI agents organized into 10 departments (Executive, Engineering, Marketing, Creative, Legal, Customer Service, IT, Product, Operations, and a Board of Directors). Each agent is assigned a specific model tier from a stack of 10, automatically routing tasks to the right model based on complexity and cost.

AI OS is open-core and self-hosted — you deploy it on your own infrastructure with your own API keys (BYOK), so you pay model costs at cost with no markup. The Community edition is free and open-source (15 agents, 5 departments). Business and Enterprise licenses are one-time purchases that unlock all 51 agents, white-label rights, and multi-tenant support. A free hosted demo is also available at aiosorchestrationlab.com for evaluation.

Lety.ai

Lety.ai is a conversational AI platform focused primarily on customer-facing chatbots. It offers a visual bot builder, integrations with WhatsApp and Messenger, and a handful of pre-built templates for common use cases like FAQ bots and lead capture. Model selection is limited — most plans run on a single underlying model with no multi-model routing. Agent count is essentially one per deployment.

Stammer.ai

Stammer.ai positions itself as a white-label AI chatbot builder aimed at agencies. Its core value proposition is letting agencies create branded AI chat experiences for their clients without coding. It supports GPT-4o and a couple of alternatives, provides basic conversation history, and allows custom branding on higher plans. The focus is breadth of client deployments, not depth of agent specialization.

MindStudio

MindStudio (from YouAI) is a no-code AI app builder. Users can chain prompts, connect data sources, and publish AI-powered workflows as shareable apps. It supports multiple models including Claude and GPT-4o, and has a growing marketplace of community-built apps. MindStudio is best suited for building self-contained AI tools rather than a coordinated agent workforce that handles business operations end-to-end.

White-Label: Who Is Really Built for Agencies?

Stammer.ai markets itself hardest to agencies and its white-label story is genuine — you can fully rebrand the chatbot UI, use a custom domain, and resell client seats. However, you are still deploying a single-model chatbot per client, not a 51-agent workforce.

Lety.ai offers white-label on higher tiers but the feature set underneath the branding is thin for serious agency work.

AI OS unlocks white-label on its Business license ($1,997 one-time, self-hosted), which means your clients get the full 51-agent platform under your brand with multi-tenant support. The difference is what you are actually handing over: a chatbot with your logo, or a complete AI operating system with your logo running the equivalent of an entire department's worth of specialized agents.

MindStudio does not currently offer white-label — apps are published in the MindStudio ecosystem with MindStudio branding.

The Self-Hosted Advantage: Own Your AI Infrastructure

AI OS takes a fundamentally different approach from the other platforms in this comparison — it is open-core and self-hosted. The Community edition is free and open-source (15 agents, 5 departments). The Business license ($1,997 one-time) and Enterprise license ($4,997 one-time) unlock all 51 agents, white-label rights, multi-tenant support, and — for Enterprise — 1 year priority support, custom agents, and priority response times.

For comparison: a custom AI agent build from a development agency in 2026 typically runs $25,000-$75,000 for an equivalent scope and requires ongoing developer fees for maintenance. With AI OS, you own the deployment outright after a single purchase, with an optional Enterprise renewal ($997/yr) to extend priority support.

None of the other platforms in this comparison offer a self-hosted, one-time-purchase model — they all lock you into monthly subscriptions.

Free Tier Comparison: Who Lets You Try Before You Buy?

AI OS offers two free paths that are notably more functional than the competition:

  • Free hosted demo at aiosorchestrationlab.com — a limited preview of the platform for evaluation, no credit card required
  • Community edition — free, open-source, self-hosted. Includes 15 agents across 5 departments with full BYOK support. No time limit, no message cap

Lety.ai and MindStudio offer free tiers with heavily throttled message limits — typically 50-100 messages per month before you hit a paywall. Stammer.ai has no free tier; the lowest entry point is a paid plan. None of the competitors offer a free open-source self-hosted option.

Which Platform Is Right for You?

Use this decision framework:

  • You want to deploy a single customer-facing chatbot quickly → Lety.ai or Stammer.ai. Simple, fast, purpose-built.
  • You want to build custom no-code AI tools and share them → MindStudio. Excellent for one-off apps and community sharing.
  • You want a complete AI workforce that handles SEO, content, code review, legal, customer support, and more → AI OS. Nothing else in this comparison ships 51 agents across 10 departments out of the box.
  • You are an agency that wants to resell a full AI OS under your brand → AI OS Business ($1,997 one-time) or Enterprise ($4,997 one-time) license, self-hosted. The white-label offering is deeper than a chatbot with your logo, and there is no recurring platform fee.
  • You want to try it first for free → The Community edition is open-source (15 agents, 5 departments), or use the free hosted demo at aiosorchestrationlab.com.
